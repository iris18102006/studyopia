# 🌱 Studyopia

> A gamified study companion designed to make learning engaging, aesthetic, and consistent.

---

##  Overview

**Studyopia** is a productivity and learning app that combines gamification with clean UI design to help users stay focused, motivated, and consistent in their studies.

Instead of boring study tools, Studyopia turns your progress into a growing ecosystem — where subjects evolve, streaks matter, and learning feels rewarding.

---

##  Core Features

###  Subject Garden

Organize your subjects as interactive “plants” that grow as you study.
The more you engage, the more they evolve.

---

### ⚡ Flashcard Battles

Test your knowledge with animated flashcards:

* Flip interactions
* Instant feedback (correct/incorrect)
* Progress tracking per session

---

###  Focus Timer (Pomodoro)

Stay productive with structured sessions:

* Work / break cycles
* Animated study mascot
* XP rewards for completed sessions

---

###  XP & Leveling System

Earn experience points through:

* Study sessions
* Flashcards
* Daily quests

Level up and track your growth over time.

---

###  Daily Quest Board

Stay consistent with small, achievable goals:

* Review flashcards
* Complete focus sessions
* Add notes

---

###  Progress Dashboard

Visualize your growth:

* Study streaks 
* Total time studied 
* Completed tasks 

---

###  Note Snippets

Quickly capture important ideas:

* Lightweight note system
* Subject tagging
* Easy access and organization

---
